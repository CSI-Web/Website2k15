PHOENIX

"PHEONIX" is the annual techno management fest organised in the month of February. Many technical and non technical events are organised A shootout to all you fucchas! Here is the list of the exciting events which is lined up every year that you get to be a part of….

Codex

A brilliant coder does not always need a problem statement. In Codex we present the participants with the executable file and they have to create the code from the exe file.

Cranium (The Coding Challenge)

CRANIUM is for all those 'techies' with the gift of creative thinking and love for problem solving. CRANIUM (Code chef) is an online as well as offline programming contest which gives you an opportunity to flaunt your coding skills in the 3 hour long intense battle (Online) and 2 rounds, Prelims and Finals (Offline).It is one of the major events that have been present since the birth of Phoenix.

Cryptex (Online code breaking event)

Absolutely NO technical knowledge is needed as a pre-requisite to participate, neither is it meant for the geeks only. A tad bit of common sense coupled with good observation, is what is takes you to enter the event that is bound to be an all-time favourite among students all over the country, like always. But to outshine them all, you need to have that 'X-factor' in you. Cryptex, very similar to a treasure hunt, is dedicated to testing not only general knowledge, but also your lateral thinking skills

Error Manic

The participants are given a particular problem statement, which one will have to code and get the maximum number of syntax errors in a certain amount of time and his partner’s work will be to correct it. Points are given according to number of errors generated and time taken to correct it, plus the numbers his partner forgets to correct.

Guess

It is an online event in which the participant has to guess the probable code of the program with the sample inputs and outputs being given to them.

Hack It

You think you are a tester? Then this event is definitely for you. Problem statement and sample code will be given; one has to hack the solution by suggesting the test cases for which the code fails.

Hackathon

Hackathon is an 8-hour programming challenge that redefines programming and arouses the mental faculties of all the programming geeks. Pitting the best of the coders against one another in this fierce battle of code, the events includes: defining, implementing and showcasing the problem statement using open source technologies. More than anything else this event is about developing something out of nothing!

Mathtrix

Chain numbers together to form correct answers and watch your math-magical powers increase. It’s time to challenge your wits; get that math geek in you out! The first round will consists of subjective problems that can be solved with applied mathematics and logic and the second round will be serious action. You will be challenged by a mathematical game that can be solved only by your mathematical and analytical skills!

Off the Cuff

The debating challenge goes to next level with online debating. The idea is about an online debating event which will follow Oxford style of debating where there is a proposition and opposition. The judgment will be done by the viewers (public) by voting for the points they find good. The one with maximum votes for his/her point will be a winner at the end of the event.

Pancratium (The Gaming challenge)

The journey never ends just as you become a pro from a rookie. So what’s next? When one of best in the country tackles to settle who is better in the gamble, we say that is the perfect display of metal. With breath taking display of gaming skills by gaming lovers from across India gaming zone gives you exactly what each and every gaming punter would like to see. Fierce competition and yes a lot of risk finally frisks the better from the pool of best. So before it says game over get your eyes and hands all over so that you beat the rest and claim to be the best.

Neuvo Gengo (The New Code)

A coding challenge with a new concept, so you think you can code? Let’s see! No C/ C++/ Java. We will give you a new language and with that you have to tackle the problem statement!

Troll it

Have Trolls always amazed you? Now do you have what it takes to amaze others? If yes, then this is right platform for you to showcase your skills. This is a unique event where you showcase your ability to think out of the box by making trolls.

Znapz

ZNAPZ is an online photography challenge in which the contestants have to click pictures and submit their entries on a given topic.

Blind Coding

It is an innovative concept that aims to test the analytical and coding skills of the students, by asking them to code with the monitor being turned off.

Every year a lot more exciting events are introduced. So guys be ready to face the challenge
